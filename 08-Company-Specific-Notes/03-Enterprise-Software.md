# Enterprise Software Notes

This archetype includes large enterprise vendors and internal platform teams.

## What to expect

- Emphasis on correctness, maintainability, and stakeholder alignment
- Security, compliance, and governance show up more frequently
- Longer hiring cycles; more process

## What “strong” looks like

- You reason about tradeoffs across reliability/security/cost
- You can operate in constraints (governance, approvals, existing systems)
- You communicate clearly with non-engineering stakeholders

## Prep emphasis

- System design with non-functional requirements (auditability, RBAC, tenancy)
- Behavioral stories that show cross-team collaboration

## Reference mindset

Cloud adoption standards and Well-Architected principles are common in enterprise cloud work.
